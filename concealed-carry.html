Concealed Carry

Carry with confidence.

Choosing to carry is a serious decision. We help you make it an informed one — the right gun, the right holster, and the right paperwork.

Permit Guidance

We'll walk you through Louisiana's concealed handgun permit process and point you to the current requirements.

Training Referrals

We connect you with certified local instructors for the training your permit requires.

Holster & Fit Consultation

The right holster depends on your build, your carry position, and your daily routine. We'll help you find it.

Before you carry

A few things every new carrier should know.

  • Permit requirements and reciprocity vary by state — always verify current law before traveling with a carry weapon.
  • We strongly recommend formal training beyond the state minimum.
  • Practice regularly, store safely, and know your local laws on use of force.

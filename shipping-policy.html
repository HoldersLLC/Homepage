Shipping Policy

Firearms

We do not ship firearms directly to consumers. Federal law requires that firearms be transferred through a licensed firearms dealer (FFL). If work is done at our shop the firearms will be picked up from our shop after payment in person.

Ammunition

Ammunition shipping is subject to carrier restrictions and state law and will also have a hazmat shipping fee. Some carriers will not ship ammunition to residential addresses, and some states restrict or prohibit ammunition shipments entirely. We reserve the right to decline or cancel an ammunition order if we cannot confirm it can be legally shipped to your location.

Accessories & Non-Regulated Merchandise

Non-regulated items (holsters, cases, apparel, cleaning supplies, etc.) ship via standard carriers to addresses within the United States. Estimated delivery times and shipping costs will be provided at checkout.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation and, where applicable, tracking information once your order ships.

Questions About an Order

Call us at 318-505-3003 and we'll help sort out any shipping questions.

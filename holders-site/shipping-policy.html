Firearms

We do not ship firearms directly to consumers. Federal law requires that firearms be transferred through a licensed firearms dealer (FFL). Any firearm ordered through us will be shipped to a licensed FFL of your choosing, or completed in person at our store, where you'll complete the required paperwork and background check before taking possession.

Ammunition

Ammunition shipping is subject to carrier restrictions and state law. Some carriers will not ship ammunition to residential addresses, and some states restrict or prohibit ammunition shipments entirely. We reserve the right to decline or cancel an ammunition order if we cannot confirm it can be legally shipped to your location.

Accessories & Non-Regulated Merchandise

Non-regulated items (holsters, cases, apparel, cleaning supplies, etc.) ship via standard carriers to addresses within the United States. Estimated delivery times and shipping costs will be provided at checkout.

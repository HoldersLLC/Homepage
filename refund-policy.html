Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: 7/10/26

Ammunition

Ammunition sales are final and non-refundable once it leaves the store, for safety reasons.

Accessories & Non-Firearm Merchandise

Unused, unopened accessories (holsters, cases, optics, cleaning supplies, apparel, etc.) may be returned or exchanged within 10 days of purchase with a valid receipt, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging.

Defective Items

If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 10 days of purchase so we can arrange a repair, replacement, or store credit, as appropriate.

How to Request a Return

Call us at 318-505-3003 or visit the store with your receipt. We're happy to talk through the specifics of your situation.
